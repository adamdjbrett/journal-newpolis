*The New Polis* is  offered by [The Whitestone Foundation ](http://thewhitestonefoundation.org/)dba Whitestone Publications, a 501(c)3 non-profit organization registered in the state of Colorado.

It is a peer-reviewed online journal published bi-annually in PDF format.  

*The New Polis* is published in partnership with the [University of Denver](http://www.du.edu/).  It not only publishes refereed academic articles, but also sponsors online webinars and seminars including its so-called "Critical Conversations" featuring international scholars.

*The New Polis* also publishes an [an e-supplement in blog format ](https://thenewpolis.com/)that pre-publishes certain articles, announces conferences, or offers content that is not necessarily appropriate as full journal articles.

The [e-pub version](http://thenewpolis.com/) has been published since February 2018.
